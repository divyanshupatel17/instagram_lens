IGLens — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-21

IGLens is a browser extension that runs entirely on your own device.

What we collect

Nothing. IGLens has no servers, no analytics, and no tracking. The developer never receives any of your data.

What the extension stores locally

To work, IGLens saves the following only in your browser (chrome.storage.local) on your device:

  • Your Instagram username and numeric user id
  • The followers / following lists you load
  • Your "keep" list and extension settings

This never leaves your browser. You can clear it anytime via Settings → Clear cached data or by removing the extension.

How it works

When you click Sync, IGLens uses your own logged-in instagram.com session to read your lists and to remove/unfollow accounts you choose. All requests go directly from your browser to Instagram — never through any third party.

Third parties

None. IGLens does not share data with anyone, and does not sell or transfer user data.
